How many Pokémon cards are there in 2026?

April 13th, 2026 via Wargamer

How many Pokémon cards are there? The trading card game has been running since 1999, with hundreds of new Pokémon cards added each year, so the number is up in the thousands. In fact, not counting promos or Japanese language cards, there are well over 17,000 Pokémon cards from main and special set releases right now - with plenty more coming all the time.

Note that there are far, far more Pokémon cards than there are unique Pokémon - due to several additional Pokémon card types like Trainers, and also because the same Pokémon can have multiple cards. If your real question is how many Pokémon are there - we've got a guide for that.

If you're still curious about the Pokémon TCG, we're also got a list of all the Pokémon sets ever released for the game - with release dates attached and special sets included.

How many Pokémon cards are there in 2026?

As of the release of Perfect Order on March 27, 2026, there are 18,349 Pokémon cards. That's counting every unique card in every mainline and special set released since 1999, including Secret Rares (but excluding holo and reverse holo variants).

The number of mechanically unique Pokémon cards is much lower than the number of cards with different collector's numbers. Many cards are printed in multiple sets, and each set contains Secret Rare and Secret Illustrator Rare versions of cards from the set, which feature exactly the same Pokémon but different art.

For more info on how those work, read our complete guide to Pokemon card rarity.

How many Pokémon cards come out every year?

Usually, between 1,000 and 1,500 new Pokémon cards are released each year. Again, that's only counting fully unique cards, and excluding the ever increasing hordes of very similar holo and reverse holo variants that get packed into each new set.

Despite a tumultuous, hype filled year of releases, 2025 was actually on the lower end of recent years for total new Pokémon cards, at 1,166. However, the trend is very much towards bigger sets; in fact, with 295 total, January 2026's Ascended Heroes was the game's biggest set ever!

In 2024, 1,215 new Pokémon cards were released across six full sets - with the biggest influx coming from Surging Sparks, which packed a whopping 252 cards when it dropped on November 7, 2024. In 2023, there were 1,470 new Pokémon cards, and in 2022 we got a total of 954 new cards added.

Back in the day, a set with more than 150 cards used to be considered quite the chonker - but if you check out our full list of card totals below, you'll quickly notice it became the norm, from around 2018 onwards, for each new main set to add over 200 unique cards.
