Content We Liked: 12th April, 2026

April 12th, 2026 via Goonhammer

Although it’s a big internet out there, the Tabletop digital universe is pretty small and spread out. In Content We Liked, we take a look at the articles, videos, podcasts, and products that caught our eyes or were noteworthy during the prior week you might have missed.

On the Internet at Large

The Ghosts of Mortua Luna by Kitbashed Gaming

Welcome back to Content We Liked! We’re starting on a roguish note this week with a quintet of piratical weirdos from Kitbashed Gaming over on Bluesky. There’s a lot to love about this command crew like their sinister radioactive glowy bits, or the clever use of a spare Votann vehicle chair as a hover-platform. But there has to be a winner and its the smoking frog dude in a tiny ufo. Peak weirdo, we love him. Give him your riches.

 

Watermelon Camo Marine by Versus

There’s a world of silly Space Marine paint schemes out there, but the ones that stand above the others are the ones that thread the needle of being silly and aesthetically balanced. This watermelon camo’d Marine by Versus on Bluesky nails that sweet spot exactly, it’d make a pretty solid scheme without the explicitly watermelon details, but works even better with them. A delicious Primaris.

 

Newest member of my Hive Fleet: Wormy
byu/TheSnekKing inWarhammer40k

This thing talks like Yoshi and you cannot disabuse me otherwise.

 

This Week on Goonhammer

Adeptus Custodes Venatari and Coronus Miniatures Reviews

On golden pinions the second wave of revamped Adeptus Custodes figures descends upon us, with the hawkmen Venatari and the Coronus hover-transport getting re-released with brand new plastic kits. Our Horus Heresy gang take a look at the new figures and how they stack up.

Goonhammer Reviews: Horus Heresy Adeptus Custodes Coronus Grav-carrier

Goonhammer Reviews: Horus Heresy Adeptus Custodes Venatari Sodality

 

Warmachine Mk4: Getting Started

Swiftblade talks on how to get into the heavy metal combat of Warmachine, with the first of our new series covering the Mk4 edition.

Getting Started: Warmachine Mark IV

 

Champions of the Wyrd: Red Wet Skeleton

Lenoon kicks off our new ongoing series Champions of the Wyrd, talking to hobbyists pushing the boundaries of the medium and championing grimdark as an aesthetic.

Champions of the Wyrd: Red Wet Skeleton

 

A Brush with Greatness: Part 1

Keewa introduces the third of our brand new ongoing columns A Brush with Greatness, a personal journey of self improvement in painting teeny tiny figures.

A Brush With Greatness, Part 1: Introductions

 

Century of the Vampire: Cronos (1992)

Bernhardt briefly escapes the pit of Young Adult Vampire Slop for a look at Cronos, the 1992 feature debut of Guillermo del Toro.

Century of the Vampire: Cronos (1992)
