Archetype

Guilliman Gladius

Thoughts

This one's interesting, in that most of the Gladius lists you see these days run triple RepEx and Wardens. Ludovico has opted instead for a Ballistus and a big unit of Bolt Inceptors, which gives the list a little more surprise power and mobility. The Bolters trade hitting power for volume, so you generally need them pointed at your Oaths target alongside the Storm of Fire Stratagem to give the +1 AP and Ignores Cover. The midtable units are still Titus and Ventris, each paired with a unit of Victrix Honour Guard, and they've got Guilliman for backup.

Thoughts

This seems like a bad mismatch for the Chaos Space Marines on paper. Those Deffkoptas are loaded up with 3-damage kopta rokkits that will make short work of the Possessed and Chosen. That said, the Chaos Space Marines list packs a ton of speed and half of the units in Darcy's list don't want to be locked up in melee, let alone against ten Possessed or Huron and his entourage. Ultimately the biggest problem for Hamish here is the lack of transports for protection - if Darcy can avoid being charged turn 1, the Kult of Speed have more than enough movement to get around screens and walls to pick off Accursed Cultists, Chosen and Possessed, softening them up just enough to blunt their damage before a melee unit can finish them off.

Those Accursed Cultists are likely the most dangerous thing in this matchup, as they can tank a bunch of ablative wounds on 1-wound models before losing any real damage output. The big advantage of the Chosen - having lots of high-AP, 1-damage attacks - is something that the Kult of Speed can handle with the Speediest Freeks Stratagem, which will give units like the Deffkoptas a 4+ invulnerable save. The Orks can afford to throw a unit or two at things like the Possessed or Chosen for a round while picking away at them with Rokkits before going in for the kill. The Bile and Huron Chosen bricks meanwhile want to brawl at midtable and the Speed Freeks just aren't trying to give them that fight. If you're the CSM player here you're trying to figure out how you can actually pin down and kill some of these units at range and unfortunately at 24" those Deffkoptas can pick on you and your big 10-model bricks without worrying so much about retaliation. The first time a unit of Koptas opens up into Bile's Chosen or the Possessed they're likely to kill five models, and that's without any Stratagem support.

It's a close matchup, and I suspect it's going to come down to mission and first turn more often than not. Because the Kult are so fast, they're likely to be just as happy going first given they can move around walls and kite CSM units.
