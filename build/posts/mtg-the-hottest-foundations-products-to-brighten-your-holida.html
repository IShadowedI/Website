MtG: The Hottest Foundations Products to Brighten Your Holiday

Clint Lienau
3 Minute Read
Nov 29 2024
Advertisement

There’s no better gift on the holidays than the gift of a shared game, except maybe a Mana Foil mythic rare.

Now that we’re all full of delicious food, we have plenty of time to check out our favorite games. The holidays are fast approaching, and you need to think about the Planeswalkers in your life. Thankfully, Amazon has you covered.

Foundations is one of WotC’s best products in a long time, especially for new players. There are so many great products to help budding Planeswalkers get started, and no shortage of great adds for intermediate players just breaking into deckbuilding. Best of all, there’s tons of chase and power cards to intice even the most experienced of players. Whether you prefer standard or want to tackle the best casual format out there, EDH, you’ll find plenty to help you out with Foundations, and Amazon has everything you need.

1. Magic: The Gathering Foundations Beginner Box

This is the perfect start for anyone just getting into Magic. The box contains two ready-to-play decks featuring all MtG colors, a handy guide to walk you through your first few turns, and a pair of playmats with all the relevant zones marked out. Once you’re ready to upgrade, there’s a collection of MtG Jumpstart packs to upgrade your decks to full 60-card standard decks. This product has everything!

Advertisement

2. Magic: the Gathering Foundations Starter Collection

Once you’ve got the basic premise of the game, or if you know how to play but want a little boost to build your first new deck, the Starter Collection is the way to go. You get a huge collection of powerful cards, like Aurelia the Warleader and Lyria Dawnbringer, plus some booster packs, a land pack, and a handy click-down life counter. Best of all, it comes with a handy storage box for all your new cards, as well as a deck builder’s guide to help you make the most of your new collection. Even if you’re a seasoned player, this collection can’t be missed!

3. Magic: the Gathering Foundations Collector Booster Box

If you really want to outshine the competition, this is the box to pick up. Collector Boosters are one of the coolest buys for hardcore fans, and the only way to get the gorgeous Mana Foil print cards. Each booster contains multiple rares, foils, and alternate art cards to add a little extra flair to your favorite deck. If you’ve got a seasoned veteran in your life who wants their deck to sparkle just a little more, treat them to this fabulous box.
