A desktop production pipeline for animated film and series — script breakdown, storyboard, animatic, 3D shot planning, asset library, render queue, and editorial tracking, all in one tool.

Local-First by Design

Your project. Your disk. Your rules.

Every project lives as plain files on your disk, in your projects folder. Nothing is uploaded, nothing depends on a network, and the schema is open so your data outlives the app.

Plain Files

Projects are folders of readable files. Browse them, back them up, version them — they are yours.

Offline-Capable

No accounts, no network calls. Open the app on a flight, in a tunnel, in a remote studio — it just works.

Open Schema

Documented, inspectable, and exportable. Build your own scripts and tools around your project data.

What's Inside

The major modules shipped with this build

Every stage of the animation pipeline, working together in one place.

Storyboard & Animatic

Multi-panel shots with layers, transitions, and timing. Sketch, sequence, and play it back as a real animatic.

Script Breakdown

Episodes, sequences, scenes, shots, and notes — structured exactly the way your production needs them.

3D Shot Planner

A lightweight scene viewport for blocking cameras and assets. Plan coverage before a single frame is rendered.

Color Script & References

Build tonal maps and reference boards per project. Keep the look of the show locked in from concept to delivery.

Tasks, Continuity & Render Queue

Track who's doing what and what's left to render. Keep continuity notes attached to the shots they belong to.

Obsidian Integration

Link existing .md notes and read them inside the app. Your worldbuilding and your pipeline, side by side.
