Help Center

Find answers to common questions about Dragon.

How do I download Dragon?

Click the Download button on our homepage to access the Windows installer from our Google Drive.

Is Dragon free to use?

Yes! Dragon is completely free to download and use. We rely on donations and support from our community.

What platforms does Dragon support?

Currently, Dragon is available for Windows 10 and above. We're planning mobile and additional platform support in the future.

How can I support Dragon?

You can support us through our Patreon at patreon.com/cw/DragonApp. Your support helps us build new features and maintain the platform.

When will new features be released?

Check our Roadmap page for our planned features. We're actively developing D&D channels, forum features, and gameworld systems.

Still need help?

Contact our support team and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
